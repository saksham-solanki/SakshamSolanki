## The Problem

A wellness startup had a strong brand and a team of certified nutritionists  - but their service delivery was entirely manual. Each nutritionist could handle 40–50 clients maximum. They were turning away customers because they couldn't scale personalized diet planning.

The bottlenecks: manual diet plan creation (2+ hours per client), WhatsApp-based progress tracking (unscalable), no data-driven personalization, and zero engagement automation (clients dropped off after week 3 unless the nutritionist personally followed up).

## The Architecture

**AI Diet Plan Generator:** Ingests user profile (height, weight, age, medical conditions, food preferences, allergies, goals) and generates a personalized weekly meal plan using a rules engine + AI optimization layer. Nutritionists review and approve AI-generated plans  - reducing plan creation from 2 hours to 15 minutes.

**Progress Tracking App:** Mobile app (iOS + Android) for daily food logging, weight tracking, progress photos, and water intake. AI analyzes weekly trends and auto-adjusts macros.

**Engagement Engine:** Automated nudges, weekly check-in prompts, streak tracking, and milestone celebrations. If a user hasn't logged food in 48 hours, the system sends a personalized re-engagement message.

**Nutritionist Dashboard:** Each nutritionist manages 200+ clients (up from 50) with AI handling routine tasks. Dashboard shows: at-risk clients (engagement dropping), plan adherence rates, and flagged health concerns.

**Tech Stack:** React Native (mobile), Node.js backend, PostgreSQL, OpenAI for plan generation assistance, custom rules engine for dietary constraints, Firebase for push notifications.

## The Results

After 6 months: 50,000+ active users, 78% monthly retention (industry average: 30–40%), nutritionist capacity increased 4x. The AI doesn't replace nutritionists  - it handles the 80% of plan creation that is formulaic, so the nutritionists focus on the 20% that requires human expertise.
