# Requirements: High-Ticket Offer Transformation

**Defined:** 2026-03-09
**Core Value:** Transform proven AI delivery capability into packaged high-ticket offers ($40K-$1M+) and close the first deal within 30 days through LinkedIn outbound and premium positioning.

## v1 Requirements

Requirements for the 30-day sprint. Each maps to roadmap phases.

### Offer Architecture

- [ ] **OFFER-01**: 3 tiered offers defined with clear names, scope documents, deliverables, and timelines (Audit, Sprint, Transformation)
- [ ] **OFFER-02**: Value-based pricing framework with ROI calculator for each tier — price at 10-25% of projected business impact
- [ ] **OFFER-03**: ICP-specific messaging documents for PE firms, funded startups, and enterprise teams — different pain points, different language
- [ ] **OFFER-04**: One-page executive summary for each offer tier (shareable PDF, no pricing — pricing revealed on calls only)
- [ ] **OFFER-05**: Viithiisys team narrative defined — honest framing: "I lead strategy and architecture, execution by my dedicated engineering team"

### Website — Premium Repositioning

- [ ] **WEB-01**: Homepage hero rewritten with outcome-focused headline, premium positioning, zero small-ticket signals
- [ ] **WEB-02**: New /solutions page replacing /consulting — outcome-framed offer ladder, "Book a Strategy Call" CTA, NO pricing displayed
- [ ] **WEB-03**: 3-5 case studies reframed around dollar ROI and business outcomes (not just technical metrics like "84% reduction")
- [ ] **WEB-04**: Anti-feature cleanup — remove all hourly pricing references, "affordable" language, generic service lists, stock AI imagery
- [ ] **WEB-05**: Client logo bar with social proof metrics (systems deployed, industries served, hours saved)
- [ ] **WEB-06**: "How We Work" section on /solutions — Diagnose → Architect → Build → Deploy → Optimize with clear process
- [ ] **WEB-07**: Pre-qualification form replacing generic contact form — filters tire-kickers before call booking

### LinkedIn — Reactivation & Pipeline

- [ ] **LI-01**: LinkedIn profile fully rewritten — headline, banner image, about section, featured section — positioned as premium AI systems partner
- [ ] **LI-02**: 7-day warmup content plan — personal, non-promotional posts to reactivate algorithm and signal return
- [ ] **LI-03**: Daily posting cadence defined — 4 content pillars (Results, Process, Contrarian, Industry Insight), 5x/week schedule
- [ ] **LI-04**: 30 LinkedIn post drafts written — first month of content ready to publish
- [ ] **LI-05**: Outbound DM playbook — connection request templates, engage-first sequences, warm-to-pitch progression
- [ ] **LI-06**: ICP targeting criteria for Sales Navigator — company size, industry, titles, funding stage filters
- [ ] **LI-07**: Daily outbound workflow — 10-15 targeted prospects/day, engage → connect → DM → call booking funnel

### Sales Process & Conversion

- [ ] **SALES-01**: Discovery call framework — diagnostic questions, qualification criteria (budget, timeline, authority, need), 70% listening structure
- [ ] **SALES-02**: 7-section proposal template — exec summary, problem analysis, solution, deliverables, timeline, investment, team & next steps
- [ ] **SALES-03**: Pre-call diagnostic questionnaire — 5-7 questions sent before discovery call to filter and prepare
- [ ] **SALES-04**: Past client outreach plan — audit all 14 case study clients for expansion opportunities, personalized re-engagement messages
- [ ] **SALES-05**: Follow-up email sequence — 5 emails over 21 days for stalled deals after discovery calls
- [ ] **SALES-06**: Objection handling scripts — pricing objections, "we can find cheaper," "need to think about it," "need committee approval"
- [ ] **SALES-07**: CRM pipeline setup in HubSpot — stages: Lead → Qualified → Discovery Call → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost

## Out of Scope

| Feature | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Paid advertising (Google, LinkedIn Ads) | Focus on organic + outbound first; no budget for ads |
| YouTube/video content strategy | Not in 30-day sprint; text content sufficient for launch |
| Course/certification programs | Future revenue stream, not relevant to closing first deals |
| Community scaling (Skool) | Secondary to direct revenue generation |
| SEO content engine (300 posts/month) | Deprioritized vs direct outreach for 30-day goal |
| Twitter/X strategy | LinkedIn is the high-ticket channel; Twitter is noise |
| Website pricing display | Pricing revealed on calls only — displaying prices attracts price-shoppers and anchors low |
| Full website rebuild | Surgical updates to existing Next.js site, not a rewrite |
| Expandi/automation setup | Manual outbound first to validate messaging; automate after proven |
| AI chatbot on site | Ironic for AI consultant; high-touch human contact only |
