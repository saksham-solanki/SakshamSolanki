# Roadmap: High-Ticket Offer Transformation

## Overview

Transform Saksham's proven AI delivery capability into a premium consulting business that closes $40K+ deals. Four phases execute in rapid sequence with overlap: define the offers, build the credibility engine (website), activate the pipeline (LinkedIn), and arm the sales process. The offer architecture must exist before anything else -- you cannot sell what you have not defined, and you cannot do outbound before credibility assets exist. LinkedIn warmup overlaps with final website updates. Sales assets must be ready before the first discovery call lands.

**Depends on**: Nothing (first phase)
**Requirements**: OFFER-01, OFFER-02, OFFER-03, OFFER-04, OFFER-05
