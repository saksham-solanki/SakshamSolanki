## Design Notes

- Opens with numbers -- the 3-6 second impression window from research is covered by the metric opener
- "That is not a pitch. That is a track record." -- reframe that builds trust before the reader's defense goes up
- Each deployment example is one sentence with a specific metric -- scannable, not paragraph-heavy
- The CRM story is the contrarian hook -- it differentiates from every "AI consultant" who only talks about AI
- Offer tiers mentioned naturally as "three ways companies work with me" -- not a sales list
- Team narrative uses the approved language from Phase 1 (dedicated engineering team, 500+ projects, enterprise logos)
- CTA is DM-based ("DM me 'audit'") per locked decision -- no "comment [keyword]" pattern
- No banned words used anywhere
- No hashtags
- Conversational operator voice throughout -- reads like a person who builds things explaining what they do
