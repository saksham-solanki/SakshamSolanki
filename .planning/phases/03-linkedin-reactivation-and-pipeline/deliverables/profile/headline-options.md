# LinkedIn Headline Options

*220 characters max per headline. Formula: Who you help + Outcome you create + Credibility signal.*
*Recommended option marked with [RECOMMENDED].*

---

## Option 1 [RECOMMENDED]

**I build production AI systems that deliver measurable ROI | 14 deployments, $2.4M in client savings, zero failures | AI Builders Club**

**Why this is the pick:**
- Opens with builder identity ("I build") -- not a title, not a role
- Includes all three proof signals (deployment count, dollar impact, reliability)
- Ends with AI Builders Club for community discovery
- 137 characters -- plenty of room to display fully on desktop and mobile
- "Production" and "measurable ROI" filter out tire-kickers immediately

---

## Option 2

**Building production AI systems for companies that need results, not demos | 14 deployments, $2.4M saved | AI Builders Club**

**Why it works:** The "results, not demos" contrast creates tension and signals operator identity. Slightly more opinionated than Option 1.

---

## Option 3

**I build AI systems that ship to production | 14 clients, $2.4M in savings, zero failed deployments | AI Builders Club founder**

**Why it works:** "Ship to production" is engineer-speak that resonates with technical decision-makers (CTOs, VP Eng). Adding "founder" to AI Builders Club signals community leadership.

---

## Option 4

**Production AI systems. 14 deployments. $2.4M saved. Zero failures. | Building AI that works -- not AI that demos well.**

**Why it works:** Metric-first approach. The numbers hit before any description. Works well for people who scan headlines quickly. Trades AI Builders Club mention for a stronger closing line.

---

## Option 5

**I build AI systems companies actually use | $2.4M in client savings across 14 production deployments | AI Builders Club**

**Why it works:** "Actually use" is a subtle dig at the AI hype cycle (most AI projects never make it to production). Conversational tone matches operator voice.

---

## Selection Criteria

| Criterion | Opt 1 | Opt 2 | Opt 3 | Opt 4 | Opt 5 |
|-----------|-------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| Builder identity clear | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| All 3 proof signals | Yes | 2 of 3 | Yes | Yes | 2 of 3 |
| AI Builders Club mentioned | Yes | Yes | Yes | No | Yes |
| Works on mobile (truncation) | Yes | Yes | Yes | Partial | Yes |
| Operator voice | Strong | Strong | Strong | Strong | Strong |

## Notes

- All options avoid generic titles ("AI Consultant", "AI Expert", "AI Strategist")
- No banned words used in any option
- Headline displays fully on desktop; mobile truncates around 120-140 characters depending on device -- Option 1 front-loads the proof signals before any truncation point
- Consider A/B testing Option 1 for 2 weeks, then switching to Option 4 (metric-first) to compare profile view rates
