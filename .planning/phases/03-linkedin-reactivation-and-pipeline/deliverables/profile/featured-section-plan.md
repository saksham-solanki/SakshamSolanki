## Ordering Logic

The three primary items follow a conversion funnel:

1. **AI Builders Club** -- Low commitment entry (free community/newsletter)
2. **Best Post** -- Credibility proof (real metrics, real systems)
3. **Website** -- Deep evaluation (full case studies, offer details, booking)

This covers all three visitor intent levels:
- **Casual browser:** Joins AI Builders Club (stays in funnel)
- **Evaluating fit:** Reads the proof post (builds trust)
- **Ready to act:** Clicks to website (converts to call or form submission)

- **Monthly:** Review click-through data on featured items. Reorder based on performance.
