# AI Opportunity Audit

**"Find the $500K you're leaving on the table."**

---

## 1. Problem Statement

Every company has operational waste hiding in plain sight -- manual processes that eat 40-80 hours per week, response times that drive customers away, and data trapped in systems that don't talk to each other. The problem isn't awareness. The problem is that nobody has quantified the damage.

Without a clear picture of where money is leaking, companies make one of two mistakes: they throw technology at the wrong problem, or they do nothing and let the cost compound. A $200K annual operational drain becomes $400K in two years as the team grows and the manual work scales with it.

## 2. What You Get

1. **Live Workshop** -- A half-day or full-day session (virtual or on-site) with your leadership and operations team. We map every major workflow, identify bottlenecks, and pressure-test assumptions about where time and money are going.

2. **AI Opportunity Report** -- A polished, data-backed document delivered within 7-10 business days:
   - Current state operational audit with quantified costs
   - Top 3-5 automation opportunities ranked by projected annual savings
   - Implementation roadmap with timelines and dependencies
   - Technology recommendations tailored to your existing stack

3. **Executive Summary** -- A one-page brief for board presentations showing the total Cost of Inaction and the projected return from addressing the top opportunities.

## 3. How It Works

**Step 1: Discovery Call (30 minutes)**
We determine if an Audit is the right move. No pitch -- just diagnosis.

**Step 2: Workshop (Half-Day or Full-Day)**
I facilitate a structured session with your team. We walk through every major process, quantify time spent, calculate costs, and identify where automation delivers the highest return.

**Step 3: Report Delivery (7-10 Business Days)**
You receive the AI Opportunity Report. We walk through the findings together in a 60-minute review session.

## 4. Timeline

**2-3 weeks** from signed agreement to final report delivery.

## 5. What Makes This Different

- **You get a workshop, not a questionnaire.** I work with your team in real time, not from a survey filled out between meetings.
- **Every opportunity comes with a dollar figure.** No vague "efficiency gains." Specific, conservative projections you can take to your board.
- **The report is a decision tool, not a shelf document.** It tells you exactly what to do first, second, and third -- and what to skip.
- **Built by someone who deploys these systems.** Recommendations come from 50+ production deployments, not theoretical frameworks.

## 6. Ideal For

**Good fit:**
- Companies with 50+ employees and $5M+ in annual revenue
- Operational complexity -- multiple manual processes, several disconnected tools, scaling pains
- Leadership preparing for board-level discussions on operational modernization
- Companies that suspect they are leaving money on the table but cannot quantify how much

**Not a fit:**
- Pre-revenue startups without established operational workflows
- Companies looking for a single chatbot or simple automation (too narrow for this format)
- Organizations without a clear operational problem to solve
