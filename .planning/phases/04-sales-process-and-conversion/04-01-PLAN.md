Create a complete discovery call framework document following the structure from 04-RESEARCH.md Pattern 1. The framework must include:

**1. OPENING (5 min):**
- Rapport-building approach (reference pre-call questionnaire responses to show preparation)
- Agenda setting script: "I'll ask diagnostic questions for most of this call. If there's a fit, I'll explain what working together looks like."
- Confirm they completed the pre-call questionnaire

**2. DIAGNOSTIC (25 min) -- 70% listening:**
- Phase A - Situation (5 min): Company context questions (team size, current tools, growth trajectory, tech stack)
- Phase B - Pain Quantification (10 min): Integrate the EXACT 5-question COI framework from `.planning/phases/01-offer-architecture-and-positioning/deliverables/04-pricing-framework.md` Section 3. Reference it explicitly -- do NOT rewrite it. Include guidance on calculating labor cost + revenue loss LIVE on the call.
- Phase C - BANT Qualification (10 min): Specific questions for each dimension:
  - Budget: "What have you allocated for this initiative?" + follow-up if they deflect
  - Authority: "Who else needs to approve this?" + mapping the decision chain
  - Need: "What happens if you don't solve this in the next 6 months?" (connects back to COI)
  - Timeline: "When do you need this operational?"

**3. PRESCRIPTION (10 min):**
- Decision matrix for recommending the appropriate tier:
  - Audit signals: Prospect is early-stage, exploring, needs internal buy-in, enterprise ICP
  - Sprint signals: Specific problem identified, budget confirmed, startup ICP or mid-market
  - Transformation signals: Multiple pain points, PE-backed, portfolio-wide opportunity
- Script for presenting COI vs investment framing: "The investment is $X against the $Y annual cost we just calculated"
- ICP-specific language variants (PE: EBITDA/margin language, Startup: burn rate/runway, Enterprise: compliance/change management) -- reference Phase 1 ICP messaging docs

**4. NEXT STEPS (5 min):**
- Always book proposal review meeting BEFORE sending proposal (anti-pattern from research: never send proposals into a void)
- Identify additional stakeholders who need to review
- Set timeline: "I'll send the proposal within 48 hours"
- For complex deals (Transformation): introduce MEDDIC framework elements (identify champion, map decision process)

**5. POST-CALL CHECKLIST:**
- Log call notes in HubSpot (deal stage: Discovery Call)
- Calculate final COI number
- Select proposal tier and begin drafting
- Send recap email within 2 hours referencing specific COI numbers discussed

Include a "Red Flags / Disqualification Signals" section: no budget authority, timeline > 12 months, "just exploring" without pain, requesting hourly rates.

Use operator-first voice throughout. No corporate jargon. No "leverage" or "synergy."

<task type="auto">
  <name>Task 2: Pre-call diagnostic questionnaire with COI-priming questions</name>
  <files>.planning/phases/04-sales-process-and-conversion/deliverables/discovery/pre-call-questionnaire.md</files>
  <action>
Create the pre-call diagnostic questionnaire document. This questionnaire is sent to prospects 24-48 hours before the discovery call. It serves two purposes: (1) filter tire-kickers who won't complete it, and (2) prime the COI conversation by getting prospects thinking about their problem in quantifiable terms BEFORE the call.

The questionnaire must include:

**Introduction copy** (2-3 sentences explaining why they're receiving this and what to expect on the call):
- "To make the most of our time together, I'd like to understand your current situation before we speak."
- Set expectation: completing this is required before the call.

**5-7 Questions** (build from the research example in 04-RESEARCH.md but enhance with COI-priming):

1. What is the specific operational challenge you're looking to solve? (One paragraph -- the more specific, the better.)
2. How many people on your team are currently involved in this process? (Approximate number and roles.) -- This primes COI Question 1
3. Roughly how many hours per week does your team spend on this process? -- This primes COI Question 1
4. What tools or systems do you currently use for this workflow? (CRM, helpdesk, spreadsheets, custom software -- list everything.)
5. Have you attempted to solve this problem before? What happened? (Previous vendors, internal attempts, tools tried.)
6. Who else will be involved in the decision to move forward? (Names and titles.) -- This primes BANT Authority
7. What is your ideal timeline for having a solution in place? -- This primes BANT Timeline

**Usage instructions** for Saksham:
- Send via email (not a form -- feels more personal for high-ticket prospects)
- If prospect doesn't complete within 24 hours, send one follow-up
- If still incomplete at call time, use the first 10 minutes of the call to walk through it verbally (never cancel the call)
- Review responses and annotate with notes BEFORE the call (3x3 research method: 3 things about the company, 3 about the person)

**Connection to discovery call:** Map each question to the phase of the discovery call it feeds. Example: Q2-Q3 feed directly into COI calculation in Diagnostic Phase B.

Note the relationship to the /solutions pre-qualification form (WEB-07): the pre-qual form on the website is a TOP-of-funnel filter (7 fields, lightweight). This questionnaire is sent AFTER a call is booked -- it goes deeper and requires more thought.
